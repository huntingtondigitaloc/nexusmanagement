Why You Should Mine

Let Us Help You
Bitcoin is mined with individual computers known as ASICs (Application-Specific Integrated Circuit)
A machine with one purpose alone.

When you decide to invest in Bitcoin, you can either buy Bitcoin directly dollar for dollar, or you can invest in mining machines that unlock Bitcoin directly from the blockchain. Both options allow the investor to own Bitcoin, but only mining gives the investor ownership in a hard asset. Through mining the investor hedges his bets, establishes a passive income stream, and invests in Bitcoin.

Mining will often yield a higher return on investment than buying Bitcoin directly.
